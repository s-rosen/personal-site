ToBI guide ↗ decoding audio… Drag the blue lines to move word boundaries (shared boundaries move together). Double-click a shared boundary to split it and open a gap (silence) between the words; drag two edges back together to re-join them. ToBI lane: drag a label to move it, double-click it to relabel (empty = delete), double-click empty lane space to add one. Click a word box to hear just that word. Click the spectrogram to seek. Save writes straight into assets/speech-data.js — then refresh the main site.
